About Me

Who I Am

I'm Prajjwal Silwal — an Automation Engineer and Data Analyst based in Monroe, LA. My work lives at the intersection of backend engineering and business intelligence. I build the pipeline, structure the data, and deliver the dashboard — so ownership gets clear answers without touching a spreadsheet.

At Braud Property Management, I designed and built the Braud Pipeline from scratch — a Playwright and Prefect-powered system that automates 17–19 operational reports, loads clean data into PostgreSQL, and drives a live analytics dashboard. When the platform had no native API, I engineered around it. When the owner needed answers, I built the analysis layer that provided them.

Before that, four years as an independent Technical Analyst gave me ground-level fluency in IT systems, hardware, and operational diagnostics across 20+ client environments. I bring both the engineering depth to build reliable systems and the analytical eye to make the data mean something.
