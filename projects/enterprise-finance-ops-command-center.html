The Challenge

Business Problem

Problem Statement

Enterprise finance and operations teams struggled with fragmented data sources, inconsistent KPI definitions, and manual reporting processes. Decision-makers lacked real-time visibility into financial performance, operational metrics, and variance analysis, preventing proactive management and strategic planning.

Solution Approach

Built a comprehensive analytics ecosystem featuring synthetic data generation, automated KPI calculations, interactive Streamlit dashboards, variance analysis, and multi-layered reporting. The solution standardizes metrics across finance and operations while enabling real-time decision-making at the executive level.

Data & Methodology

Approach & Implementation

Data Architecture

  • Fact Tables: fact_financials, fact_operations
  • Dimensions: dim_date, dim_department, dim_region
  • Volume: 3-4 years of monthly financial and operational data
  • Metrics: Revenue, expenses, profit, headcount, tickets, SLA compliance
  • Granularity: Monthly aggregation by department and region

Methodology

  1. Synthetic Data Generation (Python + Faker)
  2. Data Pipeline Development (SQL + Pandas)
  3. Exploratory Data Analysis (Jupyter Notebooks)
  4. KPI Automation & Standardization
  5. Dashboard Development (Streamlit)
  6. Automated Reporting & Insights

Sample SQL Aggregation

-- Monthly Financial Metrics Aggregation
SELECT 
    d.region_name,
    d.dept_name,
    DATE_TRUNC('month', f.date_id) AS month,
    SUM(f.revenue) AS total_revenue,
    SUM(f.opex) AS total_opex,
    SUM(f.profit) AS total_profit,
    AVG(f.profit_margin) AS avg_profit_margin,
    SUM(f.budget_revenue) AS total_budget_revenue,
    (SUM(f.revenue) - SUM(f.budget_revenue)) / SUM(f.budget_revenue) * 100 AS revenue_variance_pct
FROM fact_financials f
JOIN dim_region r ON f.region_id = r.region_id
JOIN dim_department d ON f.dept_id = d.dept_id
WHERE f.date_id >= '2021-01-01'
GROUP BY d.region_name, d.dept_name, DATE_TRUNC('month', f.date_id)
ORDER BY month DESC, total_revenue DESC;
