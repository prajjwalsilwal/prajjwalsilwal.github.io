Overview

Python automation pipeline that scraped, cleaned, and structured data on 500+ local businesses from public web directories. Processed records into a structured dataset used for targeted client outreach and local market analysis — fully automated from raw extraction through final deliverable.

Pipeline Stages

01

Scrape

Python crawlers pull business listings from public directories — names, categories, contact info, locations.

02

Clean

Normalize phone formats, standardize addresses, map raw categories to a consistent taxonomy.

03

Dedupe

Match on business name + phone to remove duplicate entries across overlapping sources.

04

Export

Deliver analysis-ready CSV and SQL table for client outreach and market segmentation.

The Problem

A client needed local market intelligence — business names, categories, contact info, and location data across hundreds of businesses — but manual research was slow, inconsistent, and impossible to repeat at scale.

The Solution

Python scraping scripts extracted records from public web directories, applied cleaning and deduplication rules, and loaded structured output into SQL-ready datasets. The entire workflow ran on a schedule with no manual steps between extraction and delivery.
